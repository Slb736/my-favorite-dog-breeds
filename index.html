My Favorite Dog Breeds

Breed: Doberman Pinscher

Description:

Doberman Pinschers are one of my favorite dog breeds because I think they look really cool, and I love their posture and pointy ears.


Breed: Pitbull

Description:

Pitbulls are one of my favorite dog breeds because I've had several, and, when trained well, they are very loyal and loving.


Breed: Labrador

Description:

Labradors are one of my favorite dog breeds because, like pitbulls, I've had multiple, and they are very well-behaved and easily trained compared to many other dog breeds.
